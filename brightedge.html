Pros & Cons of Responsive Design

    Pros
  • Better user experience – With the rising of platforms and devices, users will always get the best and consistent viewing experience.
  • One site fits all – There is no need to develop platform- or device-specific sites or apps.
    Cons
  • Performance issue – The delivered codes are the same. Unless codes are mobile-optimized and handled differently on the server side, loading time might be higher, especially when using mobile internet connection.
  • Compatibility – CSS3 is not fully supported by some devices or older browsers.
