No Exit

Redesigning the "Temporary Exit Closure" notice

My first project in the Mapping and Diagramming class at Carnegie Mellon University was to redesign a "Temporary Exit Closure" notice. The announcement was pinned next to an elevator in the Margaret Morrison Carnegie Hall to inform people not to use the north door to exit the building.

My goal for the redesign was to use information hierarchy, signs, and colors to highlight key information and help people quickly learn about the announcement from first read. The floor plan orientation and language were also considered and modified for added clarity.
