Pros & Cons of Responsive Design

Pros: One site fits all. Users will get the best user experience on any devices. With the rising of platforms and devices, there is no need to develop device or platform-specific sites or apps.

Cons: Although styling will be different to provide the best user experience for specific screen sizes, the delivered codes are still the same. Unless codes are mobile-optimized and handled differently on the server side, users might experience performance issues, especially when using mobile internet connection. Also, CSS3 are not fully supported by some devices or older browsers.
