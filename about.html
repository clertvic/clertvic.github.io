About Chaiyawut

Hello!

My name is Chaiyawut Lertvichaivoravit. I am an interaction designer. I recently graduated with a Master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon University. Prior to my journey to the US, I studied Computer Engineering at the University of Duisburg-Essen. I have worked as a software engineer in Germany and system analyst in Bangkok.

I am passionate about solving problems through design and technology to make positive impact on people’s lives. In particular, I love to design information and interaction for web and mobile products that are useful, simple, and pleasurable for people to use.
