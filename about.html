About Chaiyawut

Hello!

My name is Chaiyawut Lertvichaivoravit. I am a UX / Interaction Designer who is passionate about solving problems through design and technology to make positive impacts on people’s lives. In particular, I love to design information and interaction for web and mobile products that are intuitive, beautiful, and enjoyable for people to use.

I recently graduated with a Master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon University. Prior to my journey to the US, I studied Computer Engineering at the University of Duisburg-Essen. I have worked as a Software Engineer in Germany and System Analyst in Bangkok.
