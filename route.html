Route from Home to School

Designing a "Route from Home to School" leaflet

Mike, one of your good friends from high school is staying at your place for a couple of days. What if you could magically hand him an 8.5 x 11 paper that said “Meet me tomorrow outside my classroom at 1:30 pm and we will have lunch.” What would be on that piece of paper? How do you tell him how to get there and when to leave your place?

For this project, my design solution was to turn a flat sheet of 8.5 x 11 paper into a foldable leaflet. The foldable leaflet is more convenient to carry and allows Mike to easily follow the route step by step. Walking time was included so he knows when to leave the house. A phone number was also provided in case he gets lost.
